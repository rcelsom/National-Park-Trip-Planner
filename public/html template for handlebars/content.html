How to Use Sight

  • Map lets you search for national parks by area. Click on an area of the map and it will display the parks in that area. Each result will link to the Park Service page for that park.

  • Rules and Etiquette contains information on how to stay save and have proper etiquette in parks, including downloadable flyers to share information with other people.

  • Lastly, Find a Park allows you to search for parks that allow certain activities or have certain features, like mountain biking or parks with mountains. Submit the form and then look at the parks that fit that criteria.
